One down Seven to go.

12.21.17 • For the past couple of months I came across so much change. A new school. A new enviornment. A new group of friends. A new home. Even though home was only an hour a way it didn't seem like I was still living in the same state sometimes. College is a whole new story. It's a different way of life, a different culture. It seems like you know everyone there but you still don't really know anyone. There is so much time but still not enough time. So many emotions going on through each day. Literally living with your friends is a whole new experience. Working to fit everything into your day as well as eat, sleep, workout, and do class and extra-currculars. Making sure you call your parents at least once. Running around campus do all these activities while literally living on the edge of campus. Trying to make it back home on some weekends. Balancing everything. If you can balance everything then you can probably do college right.
